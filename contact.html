Schedule a Campus Visit

Experience Eduford firsthand. We offer personalized campus tours led by current students, information sessions with admissions counselors, and opportunities to sit in on classes.

Campus visits are available Monday through Saturday. We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability.

What to Expect:

  • Guided campus tour (90 minutes)
  • Admissions information session
  • Meet with faculty in your area of interest
  • Visit residence halls and dining facilities
  • Explore student life and activities
